User Stories
1.	As a logged-out user, I want to be able to login.
2.	As a logged-in user, I want to see the full list of courses available at Kellogg.
3.	As a logged-in user, I want to search courses by Course name of Course number or professor’s name.
4.	As a logged-in user, I want to tag the courses that I’m interested in and be able to check them later.
5.	As a logged-in user, I want to sort the courses by TCE points from high to low or low to high.
6.	As a logged-in user, I want to sort the courses by bidding points from high to low or low to high.
7.	As a logged-in user, I want to search courses by a bidding points range (e.g. 200~500 points).
8.	As a logged-in user, I want to get the bidding points of certain courses in each bidding rounds.
9.	As a logged-in user, I want to see a graph showing the bidding point historical trend of a certain course.
10.	As a logged-in user, I want to compare two or three courses in the same page.
